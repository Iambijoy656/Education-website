A learning center is a designated area within the classroom that serves a purpose of developing certain skills or knowledge in children. Physically it can be presented as a table with figurines, a color-marked area filled with various interactive objects such as balls, baskets, pictures, and so on. Learning centers can, for example, be aimed at developing an understanding of seasons.
